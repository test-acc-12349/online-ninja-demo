Best Hotels in Dubai

April 10, 2025

Dubai's luxury hotel scene offers world-class accommodations, from the iconic 7-star Burj Al Arab to the adventure-packed Atlantis on Palm Jumeirah. Notable properties include the sophisticated Armani Hotel in Burj Khalifa, the tranquil One&Only The Palm, and the Arabian-themed Jumeirah Al Qasr. Other standouts are the Four Seasons Resort, The Ritz-Carlton, Bulgari Resort, Waldorf Astoria, and Mandarin Oriental Jumeira. Each 5-star property showcases unique features, from private beaches to celebrity restaurants, offering unforgettable experiences in the City of Gold.
